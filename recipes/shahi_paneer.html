Shahi-Paneer

Description

Shahi Paneer is a rich and flavorful Indian dish made with paneer (cottage cheese) in a creamy tomato and nut-based gravy. It's a popular vegetarian main course known for its delightful blend of textures and spices.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green chili pepper, chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to preference)
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cashew nuts, soaked and ground into a paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (or full-fat coconut milk)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and green chili pepper (if using) and cook until the tomatoes become mushy, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Stir well and cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Pour in 1/2 cup of water and bring to a simmer. Add salt to taste.
  6. Add the cashew nut paste and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  7. Gently add the cubed paneer and simmer for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  8. Stir in the heavy cream (or coconut milk) and sugar (if using) and heat through.
  9. Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
